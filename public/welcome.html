The OverSTATS Olympics

Welcome, Overwatch Olympians!!


Before you lies you chance for honor and glory within the Coliseum where you will be able to test your mettle against fellow competitors in contests spanning mutliple matches as you strive for victory!

You can invite up to five of your friends for trials of skill or face down against rivals in one-on-ones, all by entering their usernames into the competitor list.

After each competitor finishes their trials, the Coliseum will pull the relevant data from each Olympian and determines how many medals each have earned before crowning the victor!

But remember, whether your opponents be friends or foes matters not, as once the Olympics end, there can only be one standing at the peak!
