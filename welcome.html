Gaming X Gladiators

The Coliseum Welcomes You

Gamers, before you lies the chance for glory; however, nothing so grand ever come so easily. Upon entering the Coliseum, you will test your mettle against fellow Gladiators in competitions spanning multiple matches and different challenges in a video game of your choosing. Whether your opponents be friends or foes matters not, as once a competition ends there can be only one standing at the peak.


Invite your friends for competitions of skill or face down rivals in one-on-ones, regardless of the systems (PC, Playstation, Xbox) a competitor uses, by entering their username into the competitor list.

Upon deciding how many matches the competition will cover (1, 3, or 5 matches), the battle mode must be determined so the Coliseum may pull the relevant data from each competitor to crown the victor.


    Battle Modes:
  • High Score Hero: The Coliseum tallies the scores of each Gladiator, the winner being the Gladiator with the highest total score.
  • King of the Kills: Points nor deaths matter not here, only how many foes you defeat does. The Gladiator with the highest number of kills claims the crown.
  • K/D Clash: The truest test of skill for any Gladiator, can you defeat more enemies than can defeat you? The Coliseum averages a Gladiator's K/D over each match, yet only the Gladiator with the best K/D average will stand the victor.
